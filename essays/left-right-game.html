The Left Right Game is a story about a road trip that follows a very specific set of left and right turns — a pattern that, if followed correctly, is said to take you somewhere else entirely. Somewhere the normal rules don't apply.

There's something in the premise that goes beyond horror. The idea that you could follow instructions so precisely, surrender your own judgment so completely, and end up lost — that's not just a campfire story. That's a feeling most people have had. The job you took because someone said it was the right path. The relationship you stayed in because leaving felt like a wrong turn.

"Left, right, left, right, left." The directions don't explain themselves. You follow them or you don't.
