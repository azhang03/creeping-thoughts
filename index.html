Creeping Thoughts

Horror stories as a way to think through real life.

Each piece in this collection begins with a horror story — a creepypasta, an online serial, a piece of fiction found in the margins of the internet — and uses it as a doorway into something personal. These aren't reviews. They're reflections: attempts to understand why certain dark stories stick with us, and what they might be saying about the way we actually live.
